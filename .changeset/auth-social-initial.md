Initial release of `@varshylinc/auth-social` v0.1.0.

- Shared sign-in system: Apple (iOS), Google (Android/web), email/password (all platforms)
- Standard Sign-In, Forgot-Password, and Reset-Password screens
- Server AuthService with AuthUserAdapter boundary, 4 `as_*` migrations
- Client React components, useAuth hook, and SOREN-callable authActions
- Mock-backed Playwright smoke gate for demo-host
